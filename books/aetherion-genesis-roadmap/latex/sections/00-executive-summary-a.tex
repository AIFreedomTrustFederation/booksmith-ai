\section{Purpose of the Monograph}

This monograph is the first research volume for the Aetherion Genesis Roadmap. Its purpose is to gather the Federation vision into a buildable Booksmith source project instead of leaving the roadmap scattered across chats, repositories, Drive files, and informal notes.

The work begins from a simple organizing principle: the Federation must be understandable by humans, inspectable by developers, reviewable by professionals, and readable by Mysterion.

The book therefore treats every major claim as something that must eventually point to a source, a repository, a manifest, a review note, or an implementation status.
