# CT012 — Infinite Song

## Setup

Imagine hearing only one musical note at a time.

One note sounds joyful.
One note sounds sorrowful.
One note sounds unresolved.

Now imagine hearing the entire symphony at once.

## Question

Does meaning arise only from sequence, or from the whole composition?

## FHQCM Interpretation

Linear time hears notes. The Eternal Now hears the song.

## Classification

Philosophical and theological concept test.
