# CT014 — The Spinning Polarity Wheel

## Setup

A wheel is painted half black and half white.

When it spins slowly, black and white are distinct.

When it spins quickly, they blur into one field.

## Question

Can distinction and unity coexist depending on scale and frequency?

## FHQCM Interpretation

Opposites may be locally distinct while globally unified.

## Classification

Symbolic analogy inspired by oscillation and phase.
