# CT013 — The Tapestry

## Setup

From one side of a tapestry, threads look tangled.

From the other side, the same threads reveal an image.

## Question

Is coincidence the moment when a hidden pattern becomes visible?

## FHQCM Interpretation

Fragmented perception sees isolated threads. Coherent perception sees the image.

## Alternative Explanations

Humans may impose patterns after the fact. This must be considered before claiming objective connection.
