# FHQCM Launch Plan

## Positioning

The Fractal-Holographic Quantum Consciousness Model is positioned as an interdisciplinary consciousness, spirituality, metaphysics, and systems-theory book. It should not be marketed as settled physics. It should be marketed as a bold synthesis and living framework.

## Primary Audiences

- Consciousness studies readers
- Spiritual seekers interested in science and faith
- Sacred geometry communities
- Panentheism and process theology readers
- AI Freedom Trust Federation members
- Writers and builders interested in sovereign knowledge systems

## Launch Assets To Create

- One-line hook
- Back-cover blurb
- Short retail description
- Long retail description
- Author bios
- Keywords
- Category candidates
- Press/reviewer pitch
- Launch email sequence
- Social posts
- Diagram excerpts
- PDF sample chapter

## Hook Draft

A visionary model of consciousness, time, fractal reality, and Christic reconciliation in the Eternal Now.

## Back-Cover Direction

This book should invite readers into a living framework where fractals, holography, quantum observation, toroidal flow, panentheism, and Christic Consciousness become a shared language for understanding reality as recursive, relational, and alive with meaning.

## Launch Rule

Do not launch until the book has passed citation review, equation cleanup, author approval, and publisher proof approval.
