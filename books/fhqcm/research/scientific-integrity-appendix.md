# Scientific Integrity Appendix

## Purpose

This appendix will be included in the book to protect clarity, credibility, and reader trust.

## Core Distinctions

### Established Science

Claims supported by reproducible experiments, accepted mathematics, or mainstream scientific literature.

### Open Scientific Questions

Areas where legitimate debate remains, such as interpretations of quantum mechanics or theories of consciousness.

### FHQCM Hypotheses

Original claims or extensions proposed by the model.

### Philosophical Interpretation

Reasoned meaning-making built from evidence, logic, and metaphysical assumptions.

### Theological Symbolism

Faith-based and spiritual language used to interpret reality.

### Poetic Cosmology

Beauty-bearing language used to communicate meaning, not mechanism.

## Publication Standard

Every chapter should identify when it moves from science into interpretation. The reader should never be forced to guess whether a sentence is a scientific claim, a hypothesis, a theological symbol, or a poetic statement.

## Model Integrity Statement

FHQCM is strongest when it is honest about its categories. It does not need to pretend that symbolism is physics. It becomes more rigorous when it names what each claim is.
