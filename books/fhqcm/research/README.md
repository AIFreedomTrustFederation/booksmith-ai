# FHQCM Research Program

This folder turns the Fractal-Holographic Quantum Consciousness Model from a visionary synthesis into a disciplined research program.

## Purpose

FHQCM must not merely sound beautiful. It must become clear enough to criticize, test, revise, and potentially falsify.

The goal is not to pretend that FHQCM is already as rigorous as general relativity or quantum mechanics. The goal is to build the scaffolding required for increasing rigor:

- clear definitions
- mathematical notation
- formal hypotheses
- thought experiments
- testable predictions
- falsifiability criteria
- empirical research pathways
- limitations and critiques
- distinction between physics, philosophy, theology, and symbolism

## Research Rule

Every major claim must be classified as one of:

- established science
- mathematical definition
- philosophical interpretation
- theological symbolism
- FHQCM hypothesis
- speculative extension
- poetic metaphor

No claim should move into the final book without its classification being clear.
