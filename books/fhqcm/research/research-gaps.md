# FHQCM Research Gaps

## Purpose

This file records what the model does not yet explain, what remains weak, and what must be researched before strong claims can be made.

## Potential Explanatory Areas

- recursion across natural, psychological, and symbolic systems
- memory and anticipation in lived temporal experience
- participation and observation as interpretive categories
- coherence and fragmentation across recovery, relationship, and systems theory

## Current Limits

- no complete physical mechanism for consciousness
- no replacement for quantum mechanics
- no replacement for relativity
- no validated measurable consciousness field
- no proven mathematical theory of fractal time

## Known Weaknesses

- risk of overextending quantum language
- risk of confusing metaphor with mechanism
- need for stronger citations
- need for operational definitions
- need for falsifiable experimental designs

## Competing Models

- standard neuroscience
- predictive processing
- integrated information theory
- global workspace theory
- relational quantum interpretations
- process philosophy
- panpsychist models
- classical theological metaphysics

## Required Future Work

- citation verification
- mathematical formalization
- statistical protocol design
- figure refinement
- peer critique
- chapter-by-chapter scientific review
