# FHQCM Source Attribution Map

## Purpose

This map prevents accidental overclaiming. It distinguishes what major thinkers actually contributed from what FHQCM borrows, extends, or interprets.

## Attribution Template

Each thinker or tradition should be documented with:

- who they were
- what they actually claimed
- primary works
- what FHQCM borrows
- what FHQCM extends
- what must not be attributed to them
- citation status

## Initial Source Map

- Actual contribution area: implicate order, wholeness, quantum interpretation.
- FHQCM borrows: hidden order, unfolding, wholeness language.
- FHQCM extension: consciousness-field and Christic synthesis.
- Do not claim: Bohm endorsed FHQCM.
