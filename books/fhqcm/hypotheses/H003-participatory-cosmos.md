# H003 — Participatory Cosmos

## Claim

Reality is better interpreted as relational and participatory than as a collection of fully isolated objects.

## Status

Philosophical interpretation inspired by quantum measurement, systems theory, and information theory. Not established as a complete physical theory.

## Evidence To Review

- quantum measurement literature
- relational interpretations
- information theory
- systems theory

## Possible Tests

This hypothesis must be broken into smaller measurable sub-claims, such as information exchange, observer framing, and system interaction.

## Falsification Criteria

If all proposed participatory effects reduce to ordinary physical interaction without any need for the FHQCM interpretive layer, the claim must be reframed as philosophy rather than physics.
