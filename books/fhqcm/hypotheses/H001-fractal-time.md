# H001 — Fractal Time

## Claim

Temporal experience and certain natural processes may exhibit recursive patterning across scales.

## Status

Speculative FHQCM hypothesis.

## Evidence To Review

- fractal mathematics
- recurrence in dynamical systems
- narrative recurrence
- biological rhythms
- memory and anticipation research

## Possible Tests

Look for statistically significant recurrence structures in physiological, behavioral, or narrative time-series data.

## Falsification Criteria

If proposed recurrence structures cannot be distinguished from noise, ordinary periodicity, or already-known mechanisms, the hypothesis must be weakened or reframed.
