# 001 — Delayed Choice Observation

Figure ID: F014

## Purpose

Help readers understand why delayed-choice experiments are used in discussions of observation, measurement, and participatory reality.

## Plain-Language Setup

A photon travels toward a measuring apparatus where the measurement choice can be arranged after the photon has already entered the system.

## Reader Should Notice

- The measurement setup changes what can be known.
- The result challenges simple everyday assumptions about path and observation.
- This does not prove that human desire creates reality.

## Relation to FHQCM

FHQCM uses this as inspiration for observer participation and recursive reality, while clearly separating established quantum experiments from broader metaphysical interpretation.

## Limits

Do not overstate. This is not proof of cosmic mind, but it is a powerful doorway into participatory interpretations.
