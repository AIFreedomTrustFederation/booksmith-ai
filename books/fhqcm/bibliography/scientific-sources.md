# FHQCM Scientific Source Registry

This registry protects scientific integrity by separating established science from interpretation and FHQCM extension.

## Required Source Areas

- Fractal geometry: Mandelbrot and later fractal mathematics.
- Quantum measurement: standard quantum mechanics, decoherence, and measurement-problem literature.
- Information theory: Shannon information and Wheeler-style information-centered interpretations.
- Holographic brain: Karl Pribram and relevant critiques.
- Implicate order: David Bohm.
- Orch-OR: Penrose and Hameroff, plus critics.
- Holographic principle and entropy literature.
- Systems theory: recursion, emergence, feedback, and self-organization.

## Claim Standard

Scientific factual claims require citations. Interpretations inspired by science must be marked as interpretation. FHQCM extensions must be marked as hypotheses.

## Review Status Values

- verified
- needs citation
- needs expert review
- speculative
- remove or reframe
