# FHQCM Theological Source Registry

This registry tracks theological, biblical, patristic, mystical, and philosophical sources used in the FHQCM book.

## Required Areas

- Alpha and Omega symbolism
- Eternal Now theology
- New Covenant and reconciliation themes
- Panentheism and process theology
- Christic consciousness and harmonic reconciliation
- Universal reconciliation traditions
- Symbolic theology and sacred geometry

## Framing Rule

Theological interpretation must be clearly identified as theological interpretation. It should not be presented as empirical physics.

## Source Integrity Rule

Do not attribute a claim to a theologian, church father, mystic, or scripture unless the citation has been verified.
