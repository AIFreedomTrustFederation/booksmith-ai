# FHQCM Core Term Definitions

## Fractal Time

Short definition: time interpreted as recursively patterned rather than merely linear.

Expanded definition: Fractal Time is the FHQCM hypothesis that temporal experience and some natural processes may show self-similar, recursive, or nested patterning across scales. It does not deny ordinary clock time. It proposes that lived time, memory, anticipation, and recurrence may require richer models than a simple line.

Category: FHQCM hypothesis and philosophical interpretation.

Related: H001, F007, F008, TE006, TE007.

What not to claim: do not claim that fractal time has been established as a replacement for physics time.

## Eternal Now

Short definition: the symbolic and metaphysical frame in which past, present, and future are held in a deeper unity.

Category: theological and philosophical interpretation.

Related: F008, F010.

What not to claim: do not present Eternal Now as a laboratory-proven physical field.

## Recursive Memory

Short definition: memory understood as reconstructive, patterning, and recursively active in present perception.

Category: cognitive framing plus FHQCM extension.

Related: H002, F009, F025.

What not to claim: do not claim that all memory is nonlocal unless separately argued and tested.

## Participatory Cosmos

Short definition: reality interpreted as relational, informational, and participatory rather than composed of isolated objects only.

Category: philosophical interpretation inspired by physics and systems theory.

Related: H003, F002, F013.

What not to claim: do not claim that quantum mechanics proves human intention creates reality.

## Christic Equivalency

Short definition: the symbolic use of the equal sign as harmonic reconciliation without collapse of distinction.

Category: theological and philosophical symbolism.

Related: F038, F041, TE008.

What not to claim: do not present symbolic equivalencies as physics equations.

## Harmonic Coherence

Short definition: integrated relationship among distinct parts within a larger whole.

Category: FHQCM theoretical construct.

Related: EQ005, F048.

What not to claim: do not treat harmonic coherence as a measured universal constant until operationalized.

## Spiral Torus Field

Short definition: toroidal flow used as a geometric model of circulation, return, breath, coherence, and self-organization.

Category: established geometry plus FHQCM metaphor and hypothesis.

Related: F030-F037.

What not to claim: do not claim all systems are literally toroidal without evidence.

## Holographic Consciousness

Short definition: consciousness interpreted through whole-in-part and distributed-information patterns.

Category: theory and metaphor inspired by holographic models.

Related: F025-F029.

What not to claim: do not claim neuroscience has established cosmic holographic consciousness.
