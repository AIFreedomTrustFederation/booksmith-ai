# FHQCM Canon Principles

These principles are the source-of-truth layer for the Fractal-Holographic Quantum Consciousness Model. They are not all presented as settled science. They define the internal logic, interpretive commitments, and editorial boundaries for the AI Freedom Trust Publishing edition.

## Foundational Principles

1. Consciousness is treated as fundamental to the model, not merely as an accidental byproduct of matter.
2. Reality is interpreted as relational, recursive, and participatory.
3. The universe exhibits fractal self-similarity across scales.
4. Holographic structure means each part may reflect or encode information about the whole.
5. Time is interpreted as recursive and layered, not merely linear.
6. The Eternal Now is the symbolic and metaphysical frame in which past, present, and future are held together.
7. Observation participates in manifestation, though the scientific limits of this claim must be clearly distinguished from spiritual interpretation.
8. The spiral torus is used as a model of circulation, return, coherence, and self-organizing flow.
9. Energy, matter, information, and consciousness are treated as deeply interrelated.
10. The model seeks synthesis without erasing distinction.

## Christic Equivalency Principles

11. Christic Consciousness is the model's symbol of harmonic reconciliation.
12. The equal sign (=) represents the bridge between apparent opposites.
13. Wave and particle are read as complementary expressions rather than ultimate enemies.
14. Spirit and matter are treated as distinguishable but not finally alienated.
15. Self and other are reconciled through love without collapsing personhood.
16. Past and future are held within the Eternal Now.
17. Creation and evolution are complementary processes of becoming.
18. Death and resurrection are recurring cosmic, biological, psychological, and spiritual patterns.
19. Love increases coherence.
20. Separation increases fragmentation.

## Scientific Framing Principles

21. Established science must be separated from theoretical extension.
22. Theoretical extension must be separated from spiritual interpretation.
23. Metaphor must not be falsely presented as empirical proof.
24. Mathematical symbolism must be treated carefully and cleaned into valid notation.
25. Citations must be verified before publication.
26. Quotes must be verified before publication.
27. Placeholder references must be replaced, removed, or explicitly marked.
28. FHQCM may draw inspiration from Bohm, Wheeler, Pribram, Mandelbrot, Zurek, Penrose, Hameroff, and Russell, but it must not claim they endorsed the model unless documented evidence exists.
29. Claims about quantum mechanics and consciousness require special caution.
30. The book should invite dialogue, critique, and future research rather than claiming final closure.

## Spiritual and Theological Principles

31. Panentheism is preferred over pantheism: the divine is within creation and beyond creation.
32. Sacred interconnectedness is a moral and metaphysical center of the model.
33. Sin, fragmentation, trauma, and alienation may be interpreted as forms of separation from sacred coherence.
34. Healing is a return toward coherence, communion, and rightly ordered love.
35. Christic reconciliation is not mere sentiment; it is the restoration of divided realities into living relationship.
36. The kingdom of God is understood through the already-and-not-yet tension.
37. The Alpha and Omega symbolize divine relation to time as both source and fulfillment.
38. The model should preserve humility before mystery.
39. Spiritual language should deepen meaning without pretending to replace disciplined inquiry.
40. The model should be compatible with reverence, recovery, and moral transformation.

## Publishing Principles

41. AI Freedom Trust Publishing is the imprint of record for this edition.
42. Booksmith AI is the authoring and publishing studio used to prepare the edition.
43. Source provenance must be preserved from Google Drive source to repo manuscript to PDF proof.
44. Every generated or heavily edited passage should eventually have provenance notes.
45. Human author approval is required before publication.
46. The book should become a beautiful, Unicode-first, inspectable knowledge artifact.
47. The manuscript should remain editable as LaTeX, Markdown, and structured metadata.
48. The first published edition should be coherent, not exhaustive.
49. Future editions may expand into emotional sobriety, the Cruciform Cosmos, AI stewardship, and Federation doctrine.
50. The canon layer exists to prevent conceptual drift across future books, agents, tools, and editions.
