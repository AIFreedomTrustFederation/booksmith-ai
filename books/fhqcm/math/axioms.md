# FHQCM Axiom Registry

These axioms are provisional research axioms, not proven laws.

## Axiom Status Values

- formal definition
- interpretive premise
- testable hypothesis
- theological symbol
- poetic metaphor

## A001 — Relational Reality

Reality is modeled as relations among systems, not merely as isolated objects.

Status: interpretive premise.

## A002 — Recursive Patterning

Some systems exhibit self-similar patterns across scales.

Status: established in fractal mathematics; FHQCM extension when applied to consciousness or time.

## A003 — Information Mediation

Observation and interaction involve information exchange or constraint.

Status: broadly compatible with science; specific FHQCM extensions require testing.

## A004 — Conscious Integration

Consciousness is modeled as an integrator of memory, perception, anticipation, and meaning.

Status: theoretical and partly compatible with cognitive science.

## A005 — Coherence Gradient

Integrated systems may exhibit measurable coherence across physiological, behavioral, and relational layers.

Status: testable hypothesis.

## A006 — Symbolic Reconciliation

Christic equivalency symbolizes unity without collapse of distinction.

Status: theological and philosophical symbol.
