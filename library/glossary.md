# Federation Library Glossary

This glossary is the shared vocabulary layer for Booksmith AI's federated book library. Individual books may extend these definitions locally, but canonical terms should remain traceable here.

## Core Terms

### AI Freedom Trust Federation
A federation-oriented ecosystem for sovereign, inspectable, local-first, human-aligned AI, publishing, trust infrastructure, and cooperative knowledge systems.

### Booksmith AI
The authoring, editing, typesetting, publishing packet, and launch studio for the AI Freedom Trust Federation library.

### Federated Book
A book that is complete as its own work while also maintaining explicit references to related books, shared glossary terms, and source lineages.

### Federation Library
The complete cross-referenced library of books, manuals, guides, white papers, constitutions, and project doctrines maintained through Booksmith AI.

### Universal Process Theosophy
A proposed metaphysical framework describing reality as one continuous process of Spirit, consciousness, matter, life, meaning, and social becoming.

### Meta-Equivalence Principle
A proposed philosophical extension inspired by equivalence in physics: apparent opposites may be locally distinguishable while globally participating in a deeper unity.

### Coherence
Patterned alignment, relationship, intelligibility, or integration within a system or field.

### Decoherence
Differentiation, fragmentation, dispersion, or apparent separation within a system or field. In strict physics contexts, use the formal quantum meaning; in metaphysical contexts, mark the term as analogical or extended.

### Greenshift
A proposed symbolic center-zone where redshift-like expansion and blueshift-like return meet as regenerative coherence.

### Krystal Spiral
A symbolic geometry for recursive consciousness, harmonic observation, memory, return, and transformation.

### Eternal Now
A philosophical and mystical frame in which all moments are understood as participating in a deeper whole beyond ordinary linear sequence.

### Publishing Packet
A reproducible bundle of manuscript, metadata, citations, cover assets, typesetting sources, export logs, launch materials, and provenance records.

### Provenance Log
A record of sources, prompts, AI assistance, revisions, approvals, citations, build commands, and export history for a book or publishing asset.
